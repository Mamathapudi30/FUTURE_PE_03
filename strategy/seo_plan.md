# SEO Plan
## Business Overview
This project focuses on a genetic testing service provider in Hyderabad. The goal is to improve online visibility and attract potential customers through SEO-optimized content.

---
## Target Audience
- Couples planning pregnancy  
- Individuals with hereditary health concerns  
- Health-conscious individuals  
- Patients looking for preventive healthcare  

---
## SEO Goals
- Rank on Google for keywords related to genetic testing in Hyderabad  
- Increase organic website traffic  
- Generate quality leads and inquiries  
- Build trust through educational content  

---
## Content Strategy
The SEO strategy is based on a pillar-cluster model:
- Pillar Page: A comprehensive guide on genetic testing in Hyderabad  
- Cluster Pages: Supporting blogs focusing on cost, types, accuracy, and target audience  
This structure helps improve search engine rankings and builds topic authority.

---
## Expected Results
- Improved search engine visibility  
- Increased website traffic  
- Higher user engagement  
- More customer inquiries and bookings
