# Genetic Testing in Hyderabad: Complete Guide (Cost, Types & Benefits)

Genetic testing is a medical test that identifies changes in chromosomes, genes, or proteins. It helps detect hereditary conditions, guide medical decisions, and support preventive healthcare.

---
## Types of Genetic Tests
- **Diagnostic Tests:** Confirm or rule out a suspected genetic condition  
- **Predictive and Pre-symptomatic Tests:** Identify risk of developing conditions  
- **Carrier Screening:** Determine if someone carries a gene for a genetic disorder  
- **Prenatal Testing:** Screen the fetus for genetic conditions  
- **Ancestry Testing:** Trace family origins and genetic lineage  

---
## Benefits of Genetic Testing
- Early detection of hereditary diseases  
- Better medical decision-making  
- Family planning guidance  
- Personalized health insights  

---
## Cost of Genetic Testing in Hyderabad
- Costs vary based on test type:  
  - Basic DNA Test: ₹5,000–₹10,000  
  - Comprehensive Health Screening: ₹15,000–₹30,000  
  - Prenatal Screening: ₹8,000–₹20,000  
- Factors affecting cost: lab quality, test complexity, turnaround time
