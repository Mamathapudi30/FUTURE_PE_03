# Types of Genetic Tests Explained

Genetic testing provides insight into your DNA, helping detect hereditary conditions, guide medical decisions, and plan for the future. Hyderabad offers a variety of tests, each serving a specific purpose.

---
## Common Types of Genetic Tests
### 1. Diagnostic Tests
- Confirm or rule out suspected genetic conditions  
- Help doctors plan treatment or management strategies  
### 2. Predictive and Pre-symptomatic Tests
- Identify the risk of developing certain genetic conditions  
- Useful for planning preventive healthcare  
### 3. Carrier Screening
- Determines if you carry genes for inherited disorders  
- Important for couples planning pregnancy  
### 4. Prenatal Testing
- Screens fetuses for genetic conditions  
- Includes non-invasive tests and amniocentesis  
### 5. Ancestry Testing
- Traces family origins and genetic lineage  
- Provides insights into inherited traits  

---
## Choosing the Right Test
- Consult a certified genetic counselor  
- Consider medical history and family background  
- Evaluate lab certifications and accuracy  

---
## FAQs
**Q1:** How long does each test take?  
**A1:** Basic tests: 1–2 weeks, Comprehensive: 2–4 weeks.  

**Q2:** Are prenatal tests safe?  
**A2:** Non-invasive tests are very safe; invasive tests carry minimal risk.  

**Q3:** Can ancestry tests detect diseases?  
**A3:** No, they are only for lineage and traits, not medical conditions.
