# How Accurate and Safe is Genetic Testing?

Genetic testing provides critical insights into inherited conditions, but understanding its **accuracy and safety** is essential for trust and reliability. Hyderabad offers certified labs to ensure dependable results.

---
## Accuracy of Genetic Tests
- Most modern genetic tests have **95–99% accuracy**  
- Accuracy depends on:
  - Lab quality and certification  
  - Type of test performed  
  - Sample collection and handling  
- Always choose labs with **ISO or NABL certifications**  

---
## Safety of Genetic Tests
- **Non-invasive tests:** Blood or saliva samples; minimal risk  
- **Invasive tests:** Amniocentesis or chorionic villus sampling (CVS) may have slight risks  
- Follow lab instructions and professional guidance to minimize risks  

---
## Factors Affecting Results
- Sample contamination or mishandling  
- Incomplete test panels  
- Rare genetic mutations not covered by standard tests  

---
## Tips for Safe and Accurate Testing
- Use certified and experienced laboratories  
- Consult a **genetic counselor** before testing  
- Verify the methodology and test limitations  

---
## FAQs
**Q1:** Can results be wrong?  
**A1:** Rarely; accuracy is usually above 95% when using certified labs.  

**Q2:** Are there risks to taking the test?  
**A2:** Non-invasive tests are safe; invasive tests carry minimal risk.  

**Q3:** Should I retake a test for confirmation?  
**A3:** Only if advised by your doctor or counselor.  

---
## Conclusion
Genetic testing in Hyderabad is both safe and reliable when conducted by certified labs. Understanding accuracy and safety ensures informed decisions and peace of mind.
